Privacy Policy

Last updated:  |  Natural Saviour

This Privacy Policy explains how Natural Saviour collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website or purchase from us. We are committed to handling your information responsibly and in full compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Business name: Natural Saviour

Country of operation: United Kingdom

Contact email: info@naturalsavior.co.uk

Natural Saviour is the data controller for the personal information collected through this website. This means we are responsible for deciding how and why your personal data is used.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary for the purposes described in this policy. This may include:

Information you provide directly

  • Your first name and email address when you join our waitlist
  • Your name, email address, and shipping address when you place an order
  • Any messages you send us via email or social media

Information collected automatically

  • Basic website usage data (pages visited, time on site) via analytics tools
  • Device and browser type for technical purposes
  • IP address (anonymised where possible)

Payment information

We do not store your card or payment details. All payments are processed securely by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. Please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy for how they handle your payment data.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes, and only where we have a valid legal basis to do so:

  • To fulfil your order — processing and dispatching purchases, sending order confirmations (legal basis: contract performance)
  • To send you waitlist communications — notifying you about new products or launches you have signed up to hear about (legal basis: legitimate interest / consent)
  • To respond to your enquiries — replying to messages or questions you send us (legal basis: legitimate interest)
  • To improve our website — understanding how visitors use our site so we can make it better (legal basis: legitimate interest)
  • To comply with legal obligations — such as keeping financial records for HMRC (legal basis: legal obligation)

We will never sell your data to third parties. We will never use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Marketing Communications

If you join our waitlist, you are consenting to receive occasional emails about new products, launches, or exclusive offers from Natural Saviour. You can withdraw this consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by emailing us directly.

We will not send you marketing emails unless you have opted in.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your data only where necessary, and only with trusted third parties who process it securely on our behalf:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase — secure database hosting for order and waitlist records (Privacy Policy)
  • Courier services — for order delivery (name and address only)
  • Email service provider — for sending order confirmations and waitlist communications

All third-party processors are required to handle your data in compliance with UK GDPR. We do not transfer your data outside the UK or EEA without appropriate safeguards in place.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Order records — kept for 7 years to comply with HMRC requirements
  • Waitlist records — kept until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete them
  • Customer account data — kept for as long as you have an active relationship with us, plus 2 years
  • Enquiry messages — kept for 12 months, then deleted

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@naturalsavior.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

Right of Access
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Right to Rectification
Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
Right to Erasure
Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements.
Right to Restrict Processing
Ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
Right to Data Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Right to Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing.
Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for marketing at any time without affecting prior processing.
Right to Complain
Lodge a complaint with the ICO if you believe your data has been mishandled.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection authority:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
Website: ico.org.uk
Helpline: 0303 123 1113

8. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyse site traffic. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function (e.g. session management). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our site. We use anonymised data only. You can opt out via your browser settings.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of essential cookies. You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time.

9. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We use industry-standard measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption across the entire website
  • Secure, access-controlled database storage
  • Limited access to personal data — only authorised members of staff can view customer and order information
  • No storage of card or payment details — all handled by Stripe

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you have concerns about a data breach, please contact us immediately.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and products are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (such as social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
